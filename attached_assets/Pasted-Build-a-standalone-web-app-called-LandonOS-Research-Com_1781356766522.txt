Build a standalone web app called “LandonOS Research Command Center.”

Purpose:
LandonOS is an AI-guided research training cockpit for Landon. It should help him learn how to do compliance and business research properly, use AI responsibly, collect source-backed information, build leadership-ready research reports, ask for help when blocked, brainstorm ideas, complete research handoffs, and level up his skills over time.

This is a standalone app for now. Use localStorage persistence for the prototype. Do not build external integrations yet. The app may later connect to Rose OS, Command Center, Company Brain, Zoho, or other systems, but this first version should work independently.

Primary user:
Landon.

Review users:
Rose, Carmen, Gregg, and leadership.

Research focus:
Compliance research and business research.

Visual style:
Create a sleek, executive, high-quality custom interface. It should feel like an executive intelligence desk, not a school dashboard or generic task app.

Use:
- Dark navy / rich blue foundation
- White work surfaces
- Silver / steel borders and dividers
- Electric blue primary actions
- Clean executive typography
- Polished dashboard layout
- Subtle shadows
- Strong spacing
- Professional status badges

Avoid:
- Yellow-heavy branding
- Beige/tan palettes
- Brown/orange palettes
- Generic purple SaaS gradients
- Decorative blobs
- Student-style visuals
- Clutter
- Lorem ipsum

Main navigation:
1. Command Center
2. Guided Research Builder
3. AI Prompt Coach
4. Research GPS
5. Research Report Builder
6. Source Vault
7. Blocked / Need Help
8. RoseOS Chat
9. Research Completed Handoff
10. Brainstorming Studio
11. Reward Center
12. Training Academy
13. Company Brain Updates
14. Settings

General app requirements:
- All navigation must work.
- All buttons that appear clickable must do something.
- Use realistic sample data.
- Allow adding, editing, deleting, archiving, filtering, searching, and status changes where appropriate.
- Store app data in localStorage.
- Data must persist after browser refresh.
- Include empty states.
- Include success messages or toasts where useful.
- Include responsive desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts.
- Include mobile navigation.
- No dead buttons.
- No broken routes.
- No overlapping text.
- No clipped labels.
- No placeholder lorem ipsum.
- Run typecheck/build and fix errors before completion.
